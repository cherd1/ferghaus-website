Product Visualisation

Photorealistic renders that show your product exactly right, without a studio booking.

Your product, in any scene you can imagine.

Product visualisation brings your products to life without the logistics, cost, or delays of physical photography. Ferghaus creates photorealistic 3D renders that capture every detail, every material, and every angle exactly as you need it.

Whether it's a single clean studio shot for e-commerce, an immersive lifestyle scene for a campaign, or packaging mockups for print, lighting, materials, and composition are all handled to make your product look irresistible. And because it's all 3D, you get unlimited variations with no reshoots.

01
Brief

Understand your product, brand aesthetic, intended use, and any specific angles or scenes you need.

02
Model

Build or refine the 3D geometry. Set up materials that match your product's real-world finishes and textures.

03
Light and Render

Design professional lighting setups and create renders that highlight your product's best features.

04
Deliver

High-resolution final renders optimised for your intended use. Revisions and variations included.

What you get

Studio control, without the studio.

Traditional product photography ties you to fixed sets, schedules, and physical samples. CGI gives you total creative freedom. Change the colour, the environment, the lighting, or the camera angle at any point in the process.

Every render is delivered print and digital-ready, at whatever resolution you need. From hero imagery for paid campaigns to detail shots for product pages, everything is produced to brand standard.
