3D CAD Modelling

From concept geometry to production-ready models.

3D CAD modelling is the foundation of modern product development. Whether you're working from sketches, 2D technical drawings, or concept images, we create precise 3D geometry that works exactly how you need it.

Our models are engineering-grade, fully parametric where required, and ready for manufacturing, further visualization, animation, or whatever comes next in your workflow. Every dimension, edge, and surface is handled with exacting standards.

Product Development

Build 3D geometry from concept to final design. Perfect for iterating designs quickly and exploring multiple directions before manufacturing.

Manufacturing Prep

Convert technical drawings to CAD models ready for CNC, injection moulding, or other manufacturing processes. Production-grade accuracy.

Visualisation Input

Use as the foundation for photorealistic renders, animations, and other visual outputs. Clean topology optimized for rendering engines.
