Services / 3D CAD Modelling

3D CAD Modelling

From concept sketches to production-ready geometry, built to specification.

The foundation everything else is built on.

3D CAD modelling is where every project starts. Whether you're working from hand sketches, 2D technical drawings, or reference images, Ferghaus creates precise 3D geometry that works exactly how you need it.

Models are engineering-grade, fully parametric where required, and ready for the next step in your workflow, be that manufacturing, photorealistic renders, animations, or structural analysis. Every dimension, edge, and surface is handled with exacting care.

What you get

Geometry that moves your project forward.

A good CAD model is more than a visual. It's the technical backbone that downstream work depends on. Every model is built clean, accurate, and structured so your engineering, visualisation, or manufacturing team can pick up exactly where Ferghaus leaves off.

Consumer products, packaging, wearables, hardware, and custom components. If it can be drawn, it can be modelled.
