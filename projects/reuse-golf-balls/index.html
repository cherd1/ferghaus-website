ReUse Golf Balls

Packaging renders, scene styling and animation for a sustainability-focused golf brand selling refurbished balls across the UK and Europe.

Client
ReUse Golf Balls
Service
Product Visualisation & Animation
Year
2024

The brief

Clean, retail-ready imagery for a brand with a purpose.

ReUse Golf Balls had a strong product and a clear environmental message, but needed imagery that could carry that positioning across their website, packaging and social channels. The brief was for clean, premium-feeling renders that gave their refurbished balls the same visual treatment you'd expect from a brand-new product.

The work covered the full packaging range, individual ball compositions, and animation sequences for use on social media. Getting the reflectivity and surface texture of the balls right was key — and doing it in CGI meant the team could iterate on colour, angle and lighting without a single studio session.
