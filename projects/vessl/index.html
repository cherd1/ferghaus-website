Vessl

Product packaging, retail displays and 3D animation for a functional hydration brand launching across the US market.

Client
Vessl
Service
3D Animation & CGI
Year
2024

The brief

Launch-ready visuals, built entirely in 3D.

Vessl came to Ferghaus ahead of their US product launch needing high-quality imagery and motion content — with no physical stock available yet. The full scope covered retail packaging renders, point-of-sale display units and short animation sequences for use across digital advertising and promotional materials.

Working entirely from CAD and brand assets, every surface, label and material was built and lit from scratch in 3D. That approach gave the Vessl team precise control over every angle and composition, and meant assets were ready well ahead of the physical product hitting shelves.
