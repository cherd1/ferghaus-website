The brief

Illuminate the luxury.

Sorores Aromatherapy needed premium product imagery for their full candle range. We created photorealistic renders that capture the warmth, texture, and luxury of their products. Each render was crafted to showcase the candle's quality and appeal across e-commerce platforms and brand marketing.

The work needed to communicate the premium nature of the brand while highlighting the product's form and finish.
