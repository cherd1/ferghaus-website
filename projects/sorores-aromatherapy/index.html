Sorores Aromatherapy

Product stills, lifestyle scenes and animation for a premium Scottish candle brand, built for e-commerce, social media and seasonal campaigns.

Client
Sorores Aromatherapy
Service
Product Visualisation
Year
2024

The brief

Premium imagery to match a premium product.

Sorores Aromatherapy came to Ferghaus needing consistent, high-quality visuals for their full candle range. Photography was expensive and difficult to control at scale, so the brief was to create a CGI library that could cover everything from individual product shots to full lifestyle scenes and seasonal content.

The work spans the complete Sorores range — hero stills for e-commerce listings, warm lifestyle compositions for social media, and short animation loops that showcase the flicker and glow of a lit candle. Everything was built to feel authentic rather than obviously digital.
