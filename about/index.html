About Ferghaus

One studio. Every project personal.

Fergus Pottinger

Founder & 3D Designer

Ferghaus was founded to bring a personal touch to 3D design and product visualisation. Rather than outsourcing to large agencies or working with templates, every project is handled directly by Fergus from the initial brief through to final delivery.

With a background in digital design and a passion for photorealistic CGI, Fergus specialises in creating compelling 3D visuals that genuinely sell products. Whether you need product renders, architectural visualisation, animation, or creative exploration via AI imagery, the studio is equipped to deliver.

Based in Ayrshire, Scotland, Ferghaus works with brands and businesses across the UK and internationally. Get in touch to discuss what you're working on.
